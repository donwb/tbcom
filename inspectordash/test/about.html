Through the use of highly trained 'Teams' consisting of a canine scent detection dog and handler we identify the presence of bed bugs in homes, apartments, planes, hotels, and anywhere else that they may have 'hitchhiked' to and nested. How We Started Doing Inspections
Our company was founded as a result of being owners/operators of hospitality properties that faced the specter of bed bugs and the uncertainty of how to deal with them on a daily basis. The fear of being sued over something that was hard to understand led to a quest for education on the best way to identify the problem early and to document a history of being a responsible operator in the event that our systems and procedures ever came into question. Furthermore, the engagement of pest control operators that had a motivating interest in finding bed bugs at our properties led to a level of suspicion of their motivation. The goal is to empower homeowners or property managers with the the knowledge about the presence of bed bugs quickly and quietly so that if a plan needs to be implemented for extermination it can be done so as early as possible.

Scent Detection Canines
The training of our dogs is identical to the training employed for the detection of bombs and drugs and we take the outcome of our inspections and follow up just as seriously. Although the accuracy rate of canine detection exceeds 95%, it is only as good as the handlers ability to communicate with clients and visually identify clues and patterns consistent with the presence of a bed bug infestation. Our dog and handler 'Team' becomes the most effective weapon in the detection of these elusive pests and having us on your team makes sure that if extermination is required that it is done effectively and appropriately. We are in the business of inspections, not extermination, and have no financial motivation to find infestations which would then provide opportunity for more revenue. Our favorite inspections are the ones where we provide nothing but relief!

Discretion
The presence of bed bugs, even the suspicion that there may be bed bugs, can be a very sensitive issue. Guest, staff, or neighbor perception is often critical to our clients. With this in mind Discovery Dogs is very discreet in what we do. No pest control trucks, no uniforms, and well thought out replies to questions placed by the people we work around. The dogs are small and well behaved and our handlers are held to a standard of conduct that contemplates the interest and sensitivity of the process and how it can be perceived by everyone involved.
